slack-feed-proxy

https://github.com/pokutuna/slack-feed-proxy
  • {{.origin}}/p?feed={url}
    remove body contents (e.g. description, summary, content)
  • {{.origin}}/p?feed={url}&diet
    remove markups from body contents
  • {{.origin}}/p?feed={url}&org={org}&channel={channel}
    enable redirector to log where clicks are from
